About

The short version.

I'm a Los Angeles–based operator with a background in customer retention, lifecycle marketing, and growth strategy. Currently at FanDuel Sportsbook, where I manage VIP retention programs that keep high-value customers engaged at scale.

Outside of work, I'm a competitive triathlete chasing sub-5:00 at Ironman 70.3 Santa Cruz, and a founder building tools I wish existed when I started training. I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of data, behavior, and performance — whether that's customer churn or race-day execution.

I grew up in Canada, studied at Western University, and eventually landed in LA. My wife Alara keeps me honest. My dog Theo gets me out the door every morning.

Projects

What I'm building.

Active · 2024–Present

TrainGPT

An AI-powered training platform for triathletes. TrainGPT generates personalized multi-sport training plans, integrates with Strava, and tracks compliance week-over-week — built for athletes who train seriously but don't have a coach in their ear.

Built on Next.js, Supabase, and Vercel. Roughly 250 users. Iterated heavily on mobile UX, calendar visualization, and plan generation quality. The product I wanted when I was starting out.
